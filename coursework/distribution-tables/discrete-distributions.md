**Properties of a poisson distribution**

**Summary**

Represented as :

$$
x \sim Po(\lambda)
$$

Read as: X follows a Poisson distribution with lambda

Probability function

$$
P(Y) = \frac{\lambda ^ y e^{-\lambda} }{y!}
$$

The expected value(mean) and variance are equal

$$
\mu = \sigma ^2= \lambda
$$
